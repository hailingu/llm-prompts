2.3x Scale Increase

Insight: Model parameters grew from 1.8T (GPT-4o) to 4.2T (GPT-5.3) in just two years.
Driver: Breakthroughs in MoE (Mixture of Experts) architecture enabled efficient scaling.
Action: Enterprises must prepare infrastructure for multi-trillion parameter models.

Near-Human Reasoning

Insight: Reasoning scores jumped from 75 to 96.7, approaching human expert levels.
Driver: Integration of Chain-of-Thought and specialized reasoning models (o-series).
Action: Shift focus from simple automation to complex problem-solving applications.

3.8x Speed Improvement

Insight: Inference speed increased from 2,850 to 10,870 tokens/sec.
Driver: Hardware optimization and parameter-efficient fine-tuning (PEFT).
Action: Leverage real-time AI interactions for customer-facing services.
