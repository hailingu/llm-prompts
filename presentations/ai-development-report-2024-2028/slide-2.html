Executive Summary

2024-2025 Retrospective & 2026-2028 Predictions

Key Insight: AI technology has experienced breakthrough progress in LLMs, multimodal AI, and agent technologies, with significant cost reductions and performance improvements. The next three years will see a steady march towards Artificial General Intelligence (AGI).

2024-2025 Breakthroughs

1. LLM Evolution & Scale

Model parameters grew exponentially from 1.8T (GPT-4o) to 4.2T (GPT-5.3), driving massive capability improvements.

2. Multimodal Fusion

Seamless integration of text, image, audio, and video became standard, enabling photorealistic generation and real-time interaction.

3. Efficiency & Cost Reduction

Inference speed increased by 3.8x while costs plummeted by 94%, making enterprise-scale AI deployment economically viable.

2026-2028 Predictions

1. Approaching AGI

By 2026, domain-specific reasoning will reach human expert levels, with cross-domain knowledge integration following in 2027.

2. Mature Agent Ecosystems

Autonomous task execution in complex environments will become the norm, supported by standardized multi-agent collaboration.

3. Hardware & Security

Widespread adoption of specialized AI chips will drive edge computing, alongside the establishment of global AI safety standards.
